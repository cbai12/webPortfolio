HarvestHero

HarvestHero is a dedicated platform crafted with a sole mission: to enlighten and engage the public regarding the critical issue of food waste. Established with a profound sense of responsibility, this website aims to educate individuals, communities, and organizations about the staggering impact of food waste on our planet's sustainability and the urgent need for collective action.

I made HarvestHero using Bootstrap framework, integrating customized styles and fonts that resonate with an earthy and organic aesthetic. Every design element was thoughtfully curated to evoke a sense of connection with nature, encouraging users to immerse themselves in an experience that aligns with the ethos of sustainability and mindful living.
